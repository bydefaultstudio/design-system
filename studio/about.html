About

ESC

We build experiences that leave a lasting impression.

A creative technology studio in London, founded by two neurodivergent creatives. Built around a belief that inclusive design should be the starting point, not the afterthought.

What we believe

Inclusive by default.

Inclusive design isn't a phase or a checklist. It's the standard. We build for the audiences advertising usually leaves out, and the work gets stronger because of it.

Different angle, different answer.

Work that shapes culture isn't built on existing systems. It comes from teams who see briefs differently and arrive at answers nobody else would.

Feelings shape memory.

The detail others overlook is the part that gets remembered. We sweat the small things, the moment a transition lands, a word change, a tap that feels right, because that's where brand memory actually lives.

Diversity of thought, by design.

Divergent thinking isn't a side note in how we work. It's the lens. Innovation comes from teams allowed to think in patterns the room hasn't seen before.

Our story

Started in a small flat in Battersea.

We started By Default in 2023. Two neurodivergent creatives in a small flat in Battersea, London, having a conversation that changed everything. Syeda said, "Why don't we start our own agency?" Erlen, never scared of a challenge, said, "Sure, let's do it." Little did we know it would be the hardest thing we'd ever do.

But it became the most rewarding. We wanted to build the kind of studio we always wished existed for neurodivergent folks. One we'd be proud to show up to every day, and one where divergent teams can thrive. A space where different ways of thinking aren't just tolerated but treated as the thing that makes the work better.

That belief gave us our name. Inclusive design shouldn't be something you opt into. It should be the default.

Meet The Founders

Co-Founder, CEO & Head of Innovation

Erlen Masson

An award-winning designer, technologist, and innovator often described by clients as a “creative genius.” As a kid, a teacher once told him, “He’ll never amount to anything.”

Erlen struggled with dyslexia growing up. Reading and writing never came easily, but discovering Photoshop that same year changed everything. Design became the language he used to communicate visually. That moment shaped his career and still drives how he leads today.

With over a decade of experience leading multi-disciplinary creative teams for culture-defining brands, including TIME Inc., Warner, and HYPEBEAST, and working on digital campaigns for Burberry, Moncler, and Nike.

Erlen has helped shape how global audiences experience digital brand storytelling in new and innovative ways.

At By Default, Erlen leads innovation, building interactive technology that lets the studio design and deliver digital experiences at speed and scale without compromising creativity. He also leads conversations with businesses and industry peers on AI in design and digital spaces, championing its role in amplifying human creativity rather than replacing it.

He drives a culture of experimentation where creativity, systems, and technology intersect, taking ideas from concept to product at speed and into scalable commercial growth.

Co-Founder & Strategy Director

Syeda Masson

Syeda begins every project with one question: “How should this make someone feel?”

A brand and business strategist who thinks in systems, patterns and connecting big-picture vision to structured execution. Mapping how culture and behaviour shifts how consumers see and engage with modern brands today.

Her decade long experience spans across luxury fashion brands, retail and digital commerce, where she’s worked for Isabel Marant, Rick Owens, ASOS, Diesel, Selfridges and supported start up brands to launch.

Those years grounded her belief in, ‘feelings shape memory’ and ‘sweating the little details’ others miss is what gets you remembered. A philosophy that continues to guide how she leads and builds brand experiences today.

Embracing her neurodiversity as a leader, she combines her divergent thinking which gives her a unique lens in viewing the world.

Today, she advocates for creative wellbeing and neuro-affirmative support systems in the workplace, to leave the creative industry a little better than she found it.

How we work

01 — Discover

Strategy and audience insight

We start with the audience, not the brief. Understanding where the brand lives across touchpoints, where the decision moments are, and what the competition isn't doing. The research that stops us building the wrong thing.

02 — Develop

Creative direction and interactive build

We lead the thinking and shape the brief before we answer it. Creative direction, design systems, and technology decisions happen together, not in sequence. We're not a production shop executing someone else's deck.

03 — Deliver

Production, integration, performance

We stay accountable after launch. Everything we build is tested, optimised, and monitored. If it's not performing, we fix it. The work doesn't end when the files are handed over.
