Our story

Started in a small flat in Battersea.

We started By Default in 2023. Two creatives in a small flat in Battersea, London, having a conversation that changed everything. Syeda said, "What if we built the studio we always wished existed for people like us?" Erlen, never scared of a challenge, said, "Let's do it. How hard could it be?" Little did we know it would be the hardest thing we'd ever do, and also the most rewarding.

We'd spent our careers in an industry built around a narrow idea of what "normal" looks like. Designed for people who read at a certain speed, process information a certain way, and move through the world without the same challenges. As neurodivergent creatives, we'd spent years adapting to systems and spaces that weren't built with us in mind. We knew firsthand what it feels like to be designed around instead of designed for.

So we stopped waiting for the industry to change and built it ourselves. The result is better work where participation is possible for everyone, not just a few. That's our commercial advantage. Inclusive design isn't an afterthought. It's our default.

Meet The Team

Co-Founder, CEO & Head of Innovation

Erlen Masson

An award-winning designer, technologist, and innovator often described by clients as a “creative genius.” As a kid, a teacher once told him, “He’ll never amount to anything.”

Erlen struggled with dyslexia growing up. Reading and writing never came easily, but discovering Photoshop that same year changed everything. Design became the language he used to communicate visually. That moment shaped his career and still drives how he leads today.

With over a decade of experience leading multi-disciplinary creative teams for culture-defining brands, including TIME Inc., Warner, and HYPEBEAST, and working on digital campaigns for Burberry, Moncler, and Nike.

Erlen has helped shape how global audiences experience digital brand storytelling in new and innovative ways.

"The difference between a brand that gets noticed and a brand that gets remembered is participation. Content builds the trust that advertising can't buy. Media companies understand this. Every brand should."

At By Default, Erlen leads innovation, building interactive technology that lets the studio design and deliver digital experiences at speed and scale without compromising creativity. He also leads conversations with businesses and industry peers on AI in design and digital spaces, championing its role in amplifying human creativity rather than replacing it.

He drives a culture of experimentation where creativity, systems, and technology intersect, taking ideas from concept to product at speed and into scalable commercial growth.

Co-Founder & Strategy Director

Syeda Masson

Syeda begins every project with one question: “How should this make someone feel?” A brand and business strategist who thinks in systems, patterns and connecting big-picture vision to structured execution. Mapping how culture and behaviour shifts how consumers see and engage with modern brands today.

Her decade long experience spans across luxury fashion brands, retail and digital commerce, where she’s worked for Isabel Marant, Rick Owens, ASOS, Diesel, Selfridges and supported start up brands to launch.

Those years grounded her belief in, ‘feelings shape memory’ and ‘sweating the little details’ others miss is what gets you remembered. A philosophy that continues to guide how she leads and builds brand experiences today.

"Inclusion isn't about designing for everyone. That's impossible. It starts with understanding who you're excluding and why, and building them in. The brands willing to do that work are the ones nobody else can compete with."

Embracing her neurodiversity as a leader, she combines her divergent thinking which gives her a unique lens in viewing the world.

Today, she advocates for creative wellbeing and neuro-affirmative support systems in the workplace, to leave the creative industry a little better than she found it.

Chief Commercial Officer

A commercial leader with nearly two decades of experience scaling revenue across global media and cultural platforms. His experience has shaped a simple belief he leads by today:

At By Default, Paul sets the direction for revenue strategy, partnership growth, and the commercial foundation that supports the studio's expansion. What we believe

Inclusive by default.

Inclusive design isn't a phase or a checklist. It's the standard. We build for the audiences others leave out, and the work gets stronger because of it.

Feelings shape memory.

The detail others overlook is the part that gets remembered. We sweat the small things, the moment a transition lands, a word change, a tap that feels right, because that's where brand memory actually lives.

Divergent thinking, better answers.

The best work comes from teams who see briefs differently and arrive at answers nobody else would. Neurodiversity isn't a side note in how we work. It's the lens.

How we work

01 — Discover

Strategy and audience insight

We start with the audience, not the brief. Who they are, where they make decisions, what the competition isn't doing. Every project begins with the research that shapes the right brief, not just the fastest one.

02 — Develop

Creative direction and interactive build

Creative direction, design systems, and technology decisions happen together, not in sequence. We shape the thinking before we execute it, so the work is led by strategy, not production.

03 — Deliver

Production, integration, performance

Everything we build is tested across real devices, optimised for performance, and monitored after launch. If it's not working, we fix it. The engagement doesn't end at handover.
