## 01. Overview

Display advertising has a trust problem. Banner blindness is real. Click-through rates have been declining for a decade. The standard response has been louder creative, more aggressive retargeting, bigger budgets. We went the other direction.

By Default developed a suite of interactive display ad formats that turn standard IAB units into micro-experiences. Swipeable, playable, explorable. Ads that earn attention by offering something worth interacting with.

## 02. Challenge

The display ad ecosystem is built on interruption. Users have learned to ignore it. Advertisers respond by increasing frequency, which accelerates the cycle. The formats themselves have not evolved meaningfully since the 300x250 became standard.

Our challenge was structural: redesign how display ads work, not just how they look. Build formats that deliver value inside the ad unit itself, not just after the click.

## 03. Creative Concept

### The Ad as Experience

We treated each ad unit as a self-contained interactive surface. Every format was designed around a single interaction mechanic: swipe, tap, drag, explore. No learning curve, no instructions needed. The interaction is the content.

The system is modular. Each mechanic can adapt to any brand, any campaign, any standard ad size. The framework is ours. The execution is theirs.

## 04. Applications

The format library spans every major IAB size. Each format ships as lightweight HTML5, runs without plugins, and works across programmatic, direct, and social placements. Performance tracking is built in: interaction rate, dwell time, completion rate, and downstream conversion.

We built custom tooling that lets campaign teams configure and deploy new creatives without engineering support. A design system for ads.

## 05. Outcome

Interactive formats consistently deliver engagement rates an order of magnitude above static display. Dwell times measured in seconds, not milliseconds. The formats have been deployed across multiple client campaigns and continue to expand as new interaction mechanics are developed.

The real outcome is a shift in how we and our clients think about paid media: not as interruption, but as invitation.
