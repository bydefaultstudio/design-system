Why Work With By Default

By Default is not just a rich media production partner.

We are a brand innovation studio that blends strategy, storytelling, technology, and commerce to build immersive digital experiences that drive measurable impact.

We don't just make ads look better. We make them work harder.

Who We Are

By Default partners with ambitious brands and media owners to design and build:

  • Rich media solutions
  • Interactive campaigns
  • Immersive digital experiences
  • Data-driven creative formats

Everything we create is designed to increase:

  • Attention
  • Engagement
  • Brand recall
  • Commercial performance

We operate at the intersection of creativity and technology — where impact happens.

Why Rich Media (Done Properly) Wins

Digital audiences scroll fast. Static formats struggle to hold attention.

Rich media changes that. It transforms passive exposure into active participation.

When built strategically, it delivers:

  • Higher engagement rates
  • Longer dwell time
  • Stronger memory encoding
  • More qualified traffic
  • Deeper behavioural insight

But the format alone isn't enough. Execution matters. That's where we come in.

Built for Attention & Interaction

We design for:

  • Scroll-stopping moments
  • Micro-interactions
  • Layered storytelling
  • Intent-driven engagement

Whether it's:

  • Playable mini-games
  • Interactive quizzes
  • Dynamic storytelling modules
  • Swipeable product experiences

Every interaction is engineered to increase involvement and brand memory.

Deeper Data, Smarter Optimisation

Standard display gives you:

  • Impressions
  • Clicks

Our builds give you:

  • Expansion rate
  • Interaction depth
  • Swipe behaviour
  • Time spent
  • Video completions
  • Engagement funnels

This unlocks better optimisation decisions and stronger performance learning.

We don't just deliver creative. We deliver intelligence.

Higher-Quality Traffic

Not all clicks are equal.

With rich media, users interact first. They explore. They engage. Then they click.

This produces:

  • More intentional traffic
  • Better conversion rates
  • Lower bounce rates
  • Improved CPA efficiency

We optimise for quality, not just volume.

This ensures everything we produce is:

Flexible & Scalable

Whether you need a single execution or a long-term creative partner — we scale accordingly.

Positioned for the Future

The future belongs to:

  • Data-integrated storytelling
  • Culturally resonant execution

By Default is built for that future. We don't follow format evolution. We shape it.

Working with By Default means:
