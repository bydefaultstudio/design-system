Rich media is not just a format upgrade.

It's a strategic tool for driving **attention, interaction, and brand impact** within Dianomi's premium environment.

It is designed for moments where impact matters.

This guide helps you position it confidently and understand its role in digital advertising.

---

## Why Rich Media Works

Rich media performs because it:

- Interrupts scrolling in a subtle, premium way
- Invites interaction rather than forcing it
- Creates micro-engagement moments before the click
- Signals brand confidence and investment

It combines **contextual relevance + visual impact** — a powerful combination in trusted publisher environments.

---

## How To Think About ROI

Rich media should not be judged on CTR alone.

Its value comes from:

- Higher engagement rates
- Increased dwell time
- Stronger brand recall
- Premium brand positioning
- Differentiation within cluttered environments

Success is measured by **depth of interaction and attention**, not just traffic volume.

---

## Core Benefits To Emphasise

### Increased Attention

- Larger creative canvas
- Motion and interaction capture focus
- Stronger on-page presence

### Deeper Engagement

- Users interact before clicking
- Encourages exploration and dwell time
- Allows layered storytelling

### Premium Brand Perception

- Signals investment and quality
- Elevates presence in high-trust publisher environments
- Differentiates from standard formats

### Storytelling Power

- Space to communicate beyond a headline
- Ideal for launches and brand narratives
- Supports visual and video-led campaigns

### Complementary to Native

- Works within Dianomi's contextual ecosystem
- Enhances, not replaces, standard native placements

---

## When To Introduce Rich Media

Rich media performs best when the objective is **impact**, not just clicks.

### Ideal Scenarios

- Product or service launches
- Brand awareness campaigns
- High-consideration categories (Finance, Tech, Automotive, Luxury)
- Strong visual creative
- Premium budgets
- Campaigns where differentiation matters

---

## Commercial Positioning

Do not position rich media as an added cost.

Position it as:

- A premium upgrade
- A value amplifier
- A brand differentiation tool
- A way to maximise impact within premium inventory

**Framing example:**

> "For brands looking to move beyond standard placements and create stronger attention, this format delivers greater interaction within trusted environments."

---

## Rich Media vs Standard Creatives

### Standard Creatives

- Static or limited interaction
- Designed primarily for click-through
- Smaller creative canvas
- Short-form messaging
- Focused on efficiency and scale

### Rich Media

- Interactive and immersive
- Drives engagement before the click
- Larger visual footprint
- Supports layered storytelling
- Designed for attention and brand impact

---

## The Key Difference

Standard creatives are built to generate clicks.

Rich media is built to generate **attention, interaction, and brand presence** — with clicks as a secondary outcome.
