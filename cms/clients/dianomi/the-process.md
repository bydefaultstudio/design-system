## Stages

| Stage | Round | Owner | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Client Intake Form | R01 | Client → PM/Creative | 1 business day |
| 2. Creative brief + kick-off | R01 | PM → Creative | 1 business day |
| 3. Creative production + internal review | R02 | Creative | 5 business days |
| 4. Client approval & build creative | R03 | Client → PM → Creative | 1–2 business days |
| 5. Final creative amends | R04 | Creative | 1–2 business days |
| 6. Trafficking in Celtra | R05 | Trafficking / Ad Ops | 1–2 business days |
| 7. Test launch + QA | R05 | PM + Trafficking + Creative | 1–2 business days |
| 8. Go live | R06 | Trafficking | 1–2 business days |
| 9. Reporting setup | R06 | PM / Reporting owner | 1–2 business days |

---

## Round Checklists

### R01: Brief

- Creative brief written
- Kick-off call
- Creative brief approved
- Asset check & review

### R02: Creative

- Design creative formats
- Creative produced
- Creative mock sent to client
- Feedback received

### R03: Build

- Further amends to creative?
- Creative revision & build
- Creative preview link
- Sent to client

### R04: Amends

- Creative QA & revisions
- Creative preview link
- Sent to client
- Client feedback received

### R05: Trafficking

- Traffic final creative
- Publish tags & share
- With client to test
- Client approved

### R06: Campaign Launch

- Set campaign live
- Reporting setup
- Complete report setup
- First report delivered
