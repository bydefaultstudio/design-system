## Creative Asset Guidance

Rich media formats are designed to create **immersive, premium brand experiences**. Because the interaction happens directly within the ad unit, the **quality of the imagery and video assets plays an important role in the overall impact of the creative**.

Providing **high-resolution product imagery, strong lifestyle visuals, and clean video assets** allows us to build a richer interactive experience that aligns with the brand and the **premium publisher environments** where the ads will appear.

Where possible, we recommend supplying **clear, high-quality imagery and well-shot video that prominently features the product**, ensuring the interactive elements feel natural, engaging, and reflective of the brand's visual standards.

---

## Core Assets (Required for All Formats)

- Brand logo (PNG or SVG preferred)
- High-resolution imagery (PNG or JPG, no watermarks)
- Final approved copy (headline, supporting text, CTA)
- Destination URL / landing page
- Brand guidelines and fonts (if applicable)

---

## Format-Specific Assets

The following are only required if these formats are used.

### Quiz

- 3–5 quiz questions
- 2–3 answer options per question
- Up to 3 result outcomes or recommendations
- Product images for each result
- Short product descriptions
- CTA and destination URL
- Brand logo
- 1 high-quality video if required (15–30 seconds recommended)

---

### Shoppable Video

- 1 high-quality video (15–30 seconds recommended)
- 3–5 products featured in the video
- Individual product images
- Product names and optional pricing
- Product destination URLs
- Brand logo
- CTA and destination URL

---

### Product Feature

- 1–2 strong hero product images
- 2–4 feature hotspots
- Short descriptions explaining each feature
- Optional secondary product image
- Brand logo
- CTA and destination URL

---

### Interactive Gallery

- 4–6 high-resolution product images
- Product titles
- Optional short descriptions
- Individual product URLs
- Brand logo
- CTA and destination URL

---

### Customisation Unit

- Base product image
- Separate assets for each variation (colour, style, material)
- Labels for each product option
- CTA and destination URL
- Brand logo
- Any required legal copy
- 1 high-quality video if necessary (15–30 seconds recommended)

---

### Gamified Unit

- Product imagery used in gameplay
- Simple game concept or interaction idea
- Reward or promotional message
- Brand logo
- CTA and destination URL
