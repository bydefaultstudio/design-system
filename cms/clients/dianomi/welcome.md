Welcome to **Dianomi Interactive**.

This hub is the central place for everything related to rich media on Dianomi, built in partnership with **By Default**.

Use this space to understand how rich media works, how to sell it, and how to get support when you need it.

---

## What is Dianomi Interactive?

Dianomi Interactive extends Dianomi's native and contextual strengths with **premium rich media formats**.

It gives advertisers the ability to:

- Tell richer stories
- Drive interaction before the click
- Increase attention and consideration
- Run campaigns in trusted, premium environments

Rich media is designed to **complement native**, not replace it.

---

## How the Partnership Works

- **By Default** designs and builds the rich media ads
- **Dianomi** serves and delivers the ads across its inventory

From a client and sales perspective, everything is delivered through Dianomi.

---

## What This Portal is For

This portal is here to help you:

- Understand what rich media is and when to sell it
- Explore available formats and use cases
- Speak confidently about engagement and outcomes
- Get support from the By Default team

---

## When to Book a Session

Book time with the By Default team if you want to:

- Sense-check a client idea
- Understand which format fits a brief
- Review previews or test setups
- Talk through performance or learnings

These sessions are informal and collaborative.
