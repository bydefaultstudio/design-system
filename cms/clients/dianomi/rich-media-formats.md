## Additional Formats

### IAB Sized Banners

Standard banners in different sizes that can take any rich media interactivity or video capabilities.

| Format | Size |
|---|---|
| MPU | 300x250 |
| DMPU | 300x600 |
| Leaderboard | 728x90 |
| Billboard | 970x250 |
| Mobile Banner | 320x50 |

### Interscroller Ads

Full-screen scroll-activated formats that appear as the user scrolls. Often include video and interactive galleries integrated into the scroll experience.

### Miniscroller Ads

Compact scrollable units optimised for mobile environments. They stay in view as users scroll through in-page content.

### Expandable Ads

Ads that expand to a larger size upon user interaction (e.g., click or hover). Great for revealing more content.

### Interstitial Ads

Full-screen rich media creatives displayed between page transitions or content load points. Ideal for high-attention brand moments.

### Sticky & Persistent Banners

Ads that stick to a screen edge (top/bottom) even as the user scrolls. Often combined with rich media capabilities like video or interactive overlays.

## Rich Media Creative Types

### Shoppable

Interactive ads that combine rich media with e-commerce functionality — clickable hotspots, product galleries — and we can also add functionality like a map where users can find the nearest store.

### Hotspot

Any creative that utilises a hotspot for engagement for any type of vertical, whether to find out more about a product or to drive views by showcasing short-form videos. Can be used for many use cases.

### Interactive Galleries

Swipeable or tappable galleries that let users browse products, destinations, or content cards directly within the ad.

### Before & After / Slider Experiences

Ads with draggable sliders that reveal contrasts (e.g., product effects or transformations).

### Gamified Units

Playable mini-game ad formats where interaction is part of the ad experience, often tied to brand or product engagement.

### 3D Creative

Rich, visually immersive units using tilt, drag, or 3D perspectives to enhance storytelling.

### Quiz / Questionnaire

Ads that include a quiz or questionnaire that can lead to different products or end frames relevant to the answers given.

## Naming Convention

### Campaigns

**Format:** Brand - Campaign Name - Geolocation - Date

**Example:** `Versace - Fall Collection - UK - 26Q1`

### Creatives

**Format:** Brand - Campaign Name - Creative Format - Creative Type

**Example:** `Versace - Fall Collection - MPU - Gallery - Hotspot`
