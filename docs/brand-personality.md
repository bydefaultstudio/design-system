# Brand Personality

## Our Brand Archetype

**The Creator**

The Creator builds original, meaningful, high-quality work. It values imagination, self-expression, curiosity and crafting and building things that didn't exist before.

**The Secondary Archetype — The Rebel**

The Rebel challenges the status quo, questions norms, and rewrites the rules — especially when those rules exclude or limit creative talents.

## Behind the Name

Our name helps set the stage for alternate perspectives that come before it. Not as the headline — but the reasoning that shapes the conversation after. It exists in the middle of a thought, a turning point, a quiet logic that sets everything else in motion.

It signals a choice, a shift, a redefinition of what comes next. It stands for intentionality. We don't accept the industry's status quo — we reset it.

The name speaks to a new era of creative work: one where inclusivity, innovation, and neurodivergent thinkers and doers aren't the outcasts — they're on the frontline.

We're here to reform what "default" stands for in creativity, culture, and media — making it broader, fairer, and accessible by design.

## Key Messaging Ideas

- By Default: The new starting point.
- A default choice.
- A Different Standard: Where accessibility fuels innovation.
- What if the default was reimagined for everyone?
- By Default: An open sentence inviting new perspectives, new voices, and new ways forward.
- We don't adjust to the system. We design a better one.
